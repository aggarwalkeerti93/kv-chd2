Our Purpose

Our Mission

To foster an inclusive and intellectually stimulating environment where students are encouraged to think critically, innovate collaboratively, and act ethically. We aim to inspire a lifelong passion for discovery and empower learners to make meaningful contributions as global citizens.

Our Future

Our Vision

To be a pioneering center of holistic education, recognized globally for nurturing innovative minds and well-rounded characters. We envision a future where our graduates lead with empathy, adapt dynamically to change, and champion sustainable development worldwide.

Our Journey

Milestones & History

A look at how we grew from a small neighborhood project into an elite international learning institution.

2012

Inception & Foundation

Kendriya Vidyalaya PM SHRI Training was founded by a panel of Stanford and Cambridge alumni with a vision to revolutionize the standard curriculum, opening its doors to 150 primary students.

2016

STEM block & Sports Complex

Constructed a state-of-the-art Science wing with high-tech chemistry, physics, and bio-labs, alongside a FIFA-grade football turf and athletic tracks.

2020

IB Accreditation

Officially authorized as an International Baccalaureate (IB) World School, expanding our high school programs and creating international student exchange collaborations.

2025

Innovation & VR Labs

Implemented a fully paperless smart classroom project and inaugurated the VR Design Lab for interactive astronomy, geography, and anatomy learning.
