Clarifications

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries regarding admissions, eligibility, and facilities.

What is the minimum age criteria for Primary Admission?

+

For admission to Grade 1, the student must be at least 5 years and 6 months old as of March 31st of the academic year. Corresponding age adjustments are followed for higher classes.

What documentation is required during admission?

+

Parents must provide: Birth certificate, school leaving certificate from the previous school, transcripts of the last 2 academic terms, 4 passport-size photographs of the student, and proof of address.

Is there an admission entrance exam?

+

Yes. Students seeking admission in Grade 3 and above undergo a brief written evaluation in English, Mathematics, and Analytical Reasoning. This helps us gauge their academic foundation and place them correctly.

Does Kendriya Vidyalaya PM SHRI Training offer school transport?

+

Yes. We have a fleet of air-conditioned GPS-enabled school buses covering the entire municipal area. Each bus is staffed with a certified driver, helper, and CCTV tracking for maximum student safety.
