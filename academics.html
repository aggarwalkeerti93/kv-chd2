Curriculum Levels

Divisions of Learning

Our educational model adapts to every stage of a student's development, combining basic structures with individual choice.

Ages 5 - 10

Primary School

Focuses on building fundamental literacy, numeracy, and social communication skills. Learning is play-based and inquiry-driven, encouraging natural curiosity and hands-on explorations.

  • • Singapore Mathematics Method
  • • Phonics & Bilingual Language Labs
  • • Junior Science Lab Experiments
Ages 11 - 13

Middle School

Transitions students toward analytical and logical thinking. The curriculum encourages project work, self-reflection, and collaborative problem-solving across subjects.

  • • Introduction to Coding & Robotics
  • • Integrated Humanities Studies
  • • Visual Arts & Design Semesters
Ages 14 - 18

High School

A rigorous university-preparatory level offering the prestigious International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma and Advanced AP options. Personalized college counseling is provided.

  • • Advanced AP/IB Calculus & Physics
  • • Thesis-level Extended Essays
  • • Dedicated SAT/ACT Preparations
